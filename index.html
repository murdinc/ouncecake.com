SUPERUSERS

Formed in San Francisco in 2013, superusers is the results of the countless hours of experimentation and discovery with synthesizers and sequencers of two friends who met in highschool. Their debut album "We Are What We Always Were" is a concept album about the troubles faced when traveling through time and space.
